Wide frequency range Sine Outout VCO built mostly on BJTs with LC Tank Drive Buffer
===================================================================================

- Stable amplitude for wide range of output frequencies (100KHz .. 2MHz)

- Pure sine waveform output (40dB harmonics)

- 4.5V power supply
